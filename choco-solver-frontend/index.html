What happens next?

  • IntVar – work with integer variables and constraints such as comparisons, sums, and arithmetic expressions.
  • BoolVar – work with boolean variables and counting constraints.
  • RealVar – work with real-valued variables and linear/constant constraints.
  • SetVar – model sets (cardinality, membership, subset, etc.).
  • GraphVar – explore constraints on graphs (e.g., node degrees).
